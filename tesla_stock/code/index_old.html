Since we now have a method to measure similarity of two songs, we can use this to determine the uniqueness of a song. In order to do this, we measure the similarity of a song to every other song in our dataset. The average similarity score of a song to all other songs will determine the uniqueness of a song. Songs which sound similar to most others will have a very low similarity score whereas songs with distinct rhythm patterns will have much higher similarity scores.

The dataset includes songs from top 100 Billboard top artists of all time and also all the artists in Billboard top 10 over the last five years. This gives us a wide variety of songs which span multiple decades in time.
